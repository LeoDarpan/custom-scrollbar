Custom Scroll bar

Learn how to customise the scroll bar on your webpage.

Scroll bars can be styled using such steps..

  • Use pseudo classes
  • Typically three are used -
    1. scrollbar
    2. scrollbar-track
    3. scrollbar-thumb
  • These pseudo classes need to be wrapped in webkit
  • The right way to declare them is - ::-webkit-scrollbar(-track or thumb)
  • Style them as normal elements after that.
  • It can be give margin-block(Top and bottom margin) but can't be given margin-inline(left and right margin)
  • That's it enjoy
